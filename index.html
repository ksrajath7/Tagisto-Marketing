UNIVERSAL CHECKOUT?

Universal Checkout is a feature salient to the Tagisto app. Unlike other commercial platforms, the consumer has the option to browse and add products from multiple stores in one cart and complete the buy at just one checkout point. The process is hassle-free, which means the prospective customer need just check if the product of his choice is available according to requirement (product colour, size etc.) while window shopping. This reduces the chances of basket abandonment which comes about if the consumer has to browse from one site to the next repeating the exercise while making purchases.
